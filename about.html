"Who are you?"

I'm Glitchy. I could also be refered too as Davey or Error. I am a black, queer, multifandom artist, however I do dabble in a bit of writing.

I am agender, bisexual, and use she/them pronouns.

"Why this?"

To put it simply, I wanted to make my own website where I could archive and share my own works.

Every fanfic site that I use, once used, or heard of, has something about it that makes me wish to not use it, so I decided "Heh, why not just make my own?" So that's what I did!

I still cross-post to Wattpad and Tumblr. Also looking for sites for other places to cross-post on.
